Cincomancia is a helper for Cinco Paus.

Click to cycle through statuses.

TEAL means tested absent.

RED means present. Click a slot to assign. Click the numeral to reassign.

BLUE means ruled out by exclusion and sequence rules.

LIGHT BLUE means ruled out for at least half of the remaining slots.

PURPLE means forgotten. You can use it temporarily to visualize consequences.

LIGHT PURPLE means ruled out for slots that have not been forgotten.
